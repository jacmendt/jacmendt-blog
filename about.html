About

Jacmendt is one of the three Co-Founders of PIKOBYTES, a company which develops solutions for sensor driven and environmental data applications. Before that jacmendt worked for the Saxony State and University Library (SLUB) in Dresden as a Project Manager and Web Developer focussed on mapping and infrastructure applications. Beside that he worked as Consultant in different areas of spatial it and software development. My main areas of interest are Spatial Data, Web Development and Sensor Networks, with a strong focus on business development. When I'm not working I really like to play Basketball and do some Music.
